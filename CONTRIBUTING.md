Contributing
Contributions are welcome only in the following forms:
⦁	Bug fixes or improvements to toy notebooks
⦁	Corrections to documentation clarity or formatting
⦁	Experimental protocol clarifications
⦁	Reproducibility or falsification-oriented tests
Not Accepted
⦁	Philosophical debates
⦁	Claims of proof or validation
⦁	Speculative extensions without falsification criteria
All contributions must preserve the falsifiability-first framing of the project.
